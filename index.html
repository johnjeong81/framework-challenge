Welcome to HelpToStay!

We're here to help anyone in need to find a place to stay. If you are in Lansing, Grand Rapids, Detroit, or Traverse City area, we are able to provide you a place you can call home :)

Check Availability! →

What does HelpToStay offer?

We don't just offer a place to stay, we offer much more so that you can feel like home :)

Free Meal Plan and More Free Food

We offer you one free meal per day and also provide free snacks and canned foods!

Recreational Programs

We offer you multiple recreational programs like card games or indoor sports! We also have a gym you can use all you want.

Job Opportunities

We have weekly job fairs and job matching programs ready for you. There are 100+ employers looking for new workers!

Our Bunk Bed/Bedroom options

We offer bunk beds with bedsheets as well as private bedrooms! Also, we do bunkmates matching and family matching program so that your stay can be more comfortable!

We have two options of bedrooms.

  • Bunker Option
  • Private Bedroom Option (Please note that private bedroom option is extremely limited and the priority goes to families that have 3+ members)
